Frequently Asked Questions

Do Gold members automatically earn bonus points?

Only qualifying rentals will be eligible for bonus points. Member must select to receive Gold Plus Rewards points on reservation. The promo code PC 203497 is required at time of reservation. Valid after completing your rental between 15 March and 15 April 2017.


Who may participate in the CarX promotion?

The CarX promotion is valid for select Gold Plus Rewards members.

Are advance reservations required?

Yes.

Is there a minimum rental length or car class required to earn the bonus points?

Any car class qualifies for this promotion. Members must make at least one qualifying rentals during the qualification period to earn 800 bonus points.

If a reservation is changed prior to a rental, will the member still earn his/her bonus points?

If a member modifies his/her reservation, the change may result in a change in his/her rental rate and/or invalidate this offer.

Are there rental rates that do not qualify for this promotion?

All CarX Gold Plus Rewards rules apply.

When will members receive their bonus points? How soon can they use them?

This is not a real-time bonus. Points earned from this promotion will be awarded retroactively. The bonus will be issued after the transaction is received during the promotional time frame. Bonus points can be added to the member's account up to four weeks from the end of the promotion.

Is there a maximum number of bonus points that can be earned during this promotion?

This offer will award a maximum of 800 Gold Plus Rewards points.

Can members who use a corporate booking tool take advantage of this promotion?

Yes, as long as the corporate booking tool includes the Gold Plus Rewards member number and Gold Plus Rewards points in place of any other frequent travel programme points.

What are the participating renting countries for this promotion?

Rentals must be picked up at a participating location in the following point-earning countries: United States, Canada, Puerto Rico, St. Thomas USVI, Australia, New Zealand, Brazil, United Kingdom, France, Germany, Norway, Denmark, Sweden, Finland, Ireland, Italy, Spain, Belgium, Luxembourg and the Netherlands.
